WOLVERINE and X-Men are trademarks of Marvel Comics

To use this model for Half-Life simply create a new folder named 'wolverine' (NO QUOTES!) in: 
...\Half-Life\valve\models\player\...
Un-zip the wolverine.mdl and wolverine.bmp into the new wolverine folder
The X-Men symbol (x-men1a.bmp) should be cut, then pasted into:
...\Half-Life\logos

Feel free to distribute this .zip along with the README.txt
